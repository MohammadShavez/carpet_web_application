Material

Carpets can be made from natural or synthetic materials. Natural materials include wool, silk, jute, and cotton, while synthetic materials include nylon, polyester, polypropylene, and acrylic

Types

Carpets can be woven or tufted, and the pile can be plush or Berber. Plush carpets have a cut pile, while Berber carpets have a loop pile.

Dyes

Natural dyes were used from the earliest times until the late 19th century. Synthetic aniline dyes became popular in the 19th century, but natural dyes have regained favor with many craftsmen.

Insulation

Carpets provide an extra layer of insulation and warmth to the floor.

Allergies

Many modern carpets are made with hypoallergenic materials that trap dust and allergens.
